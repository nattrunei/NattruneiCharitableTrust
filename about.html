About Nattrunei Charitable Trust

Nattrunei Charitable Trust is a beacon of hope and compassion, established with the core mission of uplifting the lives of the underprivileged and bringing meaningful change to society. Rooted in the values of service, empathy, and equality, our trust is committed to providing educational support, medical assistance, and essential aid to those in dire need.

Our primary focus lies in:

  • Educational Assistance: Supporting children and youth from economically challenged backgrounds by providing school supplies, scholarships, tuition fees, and mentorship, helping them build a brighter future through learning.
  • Medical Help: Assisting poor families and individuals with access to medical treatment, medicines, health camps, and emergency support, ensuring no one is denied the right to health due to financial constraints.
  • Social Welfare: Responding to the day-to-day needs of the very poor—offering food, clothing, and basic necessities—especially during times of crisis and calamity.

As a part of our humanitarian efforts, we also run a dedicated blood donation group, connecting voluntary donors with patients in urgent need. This life-saving initiative has already made a significant difference in critical situations, saving lives through timely support.

At Nattrunei Charitable Trust, we believe that small acts of kindness can create ripples of change. With the support of generous donors, passionate volunteers, and community members, we continue to strive toward a more inclusive, caring, and empowered society.

Join us in our journey of service. Together, we can transform lives—one act of compassion at a time.

About Nattrunei Founder - Beena B

Introduction

As I traverse the path of life, I find fulfillment in three core aspects that define my identity - education, social service, and spirituality. Each of these components has played a significant role in shaping my character and guiding me towards a purposeful existence.

Educational Qualification

I believe that education is a lifelong pursuit that empowers us with knowledge and understanding. With a strong academic foundation, I have earned Msc in Psychology and MBA in HR. Education has broadened my horizons, taught me critical thinking, and instilled a sense of curiosity that continuously fuels my desire to learn and grow.

Work Experience

My professional journey has been one of continuous growth, evolution, and embracing diverse opportunities. Starting as a medical transcriptionist, I embarked on a path that allowed me to explore different roles within the healthcare industry and eventually transition into business development and management on cloud computing and mobile app development. Today, I am proud to serve as the Board of Directors Office Manager, where I contribute my skills and experiences to drive organizational success.

Social Services

Blood Donation

In my journey of giving back to society, blood donation holds a special place in my heart. I have always been deeply moved by the impact a single unit of blood can have on saving lives. Regularly engaging in blood donation drives, I have had the privilege of contributing to numerous life-saving endeavors. The sense of purpose and joy I experience each time I donate is unparalleled. One act of kindness can make a profound difference in someone else's life, and this conviction has driven me to be an advocate for blood donation, encouraging others to join this noble cause.

Wildlife and Nature Conservation

My passion for wildlife conservation stems from a profound appreciation for the intricate balance of ecosystems and the diverse beauty of our planet's flora and fauna. Volunteering at wildlife sanctuaries, participating in awareness campaigns, and supporting conservation organizations have opened my eyes to the urgent need to protect endangered species and their habitats. By engaging in wildlife conservation, I strive to be a custodian of our planet, leaving behind a better world for generations to come.

Spirituality

Beyond the material aspects of life, I find grounding in spirituality. It is a deeply personal journey that has helped me seek inner peace and harmony. My spiritual practices, be it prayer or mindfulness, enable me to connect with my inner self and understand the interconnectedness of all living beings. Spirituality has taught me empathy, compassion, and the significance of selfless service. It is the guiding light that keeps me centered and aligned with my values, propelling me to extend a helping hand to those in need.
